# Image Library

Web-optimized assets for the RD Builds Motorsport website. Each photo was
re-encoded from the originals in `../../images/` to WebP (primary) and JPG
(fallback), auto-oriented, stripped of EXIF, capped at 2048px on the long
edge, quality 82.

Use the `.webp` file in `<picture>` sources and the `.jpg` as the `<img>`
fallback. Originals are preserved untouched in `/images/` for future
re-exports.

## Drift action shots

### `drift-action-pakelo-track.{webp,jpg}`
Green BMW E46 (Pakelo / RD Builds Motorsport livery, car #7) sliding in
front of a packed crowd at a "Red Drift" event. Smoke pouring off the rear
tyres, photographers and spectators visible behind concrete barriers. Strong
hero candidate — wide landscape framing with the car positioned just left of
centre.

### `drift-panning-redrift-26.{webp,jpg}`
Panning shot of the same car (here marked #26) leaning into a corner on a
tree-lined street circuit. Background is heavily motion-blurred while the
car stays sharp — gives a strong sense of speed. Good for a hero band or a
"What we do" section header.

### `drift-cone-hit-smoke.{webp,jpg}`
Front three-quarter of the car clipping a track marker against a concrete
barrier, huge plume of tyre smoke behind. The most dramatic of the action
set. Use it where you want grit / motorsport energy rather than a clean
glamour shot.

## Static car shots (urban night)

### `bmw-e46-night-front.{webp,jpg}`
Portrait orientation. Car parked on an empty boulevard at dusk/blue hour,
street lamps lit, front three-quarter view showing the full livery and
bodykit. Clean enough to use as a build / "the car" feature image.

### `bmw-e46-night-rear.{webp,jpg}`
Portrait orientation. Rear three-quarter at night with a city tram stop and
apartment block in the background. Driver name "Andrei Cristea" visible on
the rear quarter glass. Sponsor decals (Pakelo, RD Builds, Hidro Transfer,
Temco, Best, Wolf, Digital M.O.B.) clearly legible — useful for a sponsors
section.

### `bmw-e46-night-side.{webp,jpg}`
Portrait orientation. Pure side profile at night, neon-lit storefronts
("Sushi Han", "Mămăliguță", "Irene Optic") behind. Best image in the set
for showing the full silhouette and livery layout end-to-end.

## Team & community

### `team-podium-redbull-trophy.{webp,jpg}`
Portrait orientation. Five team members on a Red Bull podium holding a
trophy, one in racing suit centre frame. Romanian flag bunting overhead.
Use for results / press / "About the team" sections.

### `team-group-photo.{webp,jpg}`
Large group shot of team, drivers and supporters in front of what appears
to be the Palace of the Parliament in Bucharest, golden-hour light. Good
for a community / fans block or an end-of-page footer hero.

### `team-paddock-group.{webp,jpg}`
Team and family group photo in the event paddock — Pakelo and RD Builds
Motorsport banners flanking the shot, blue team tent behind, kids included.
Overcast light, candid energy (peace signs, fists in the air). Best for a
"community" or "events" block where the human / family side of the team
matters more than the cars.

### `fans-supporters-portrait.{webp,jpg}`
Two supporters in RD Builds caps and Pakelo / RD Builds tees at an outdoor
event, one giving a peace sign. Strong human-element shot for a merch,
community, or testimonials section.
